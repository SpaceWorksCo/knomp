SpaceWorks Mining Pool
The official mining pool of Spacecoin (SPACE).

3% pool fee. Minimum payouts of 0.3 SPACE every 30 minutes.
The high 3% fee is to encourage other pools to be setup and help grow the network.

For support, please visit the #pool-support channel on discord.
